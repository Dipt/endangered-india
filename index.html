About Endangered India

A side project made by Dipt Chaudhary intended to spread awareness about 30 endangered species. Some of these species were once found in India with last sightings years ago and some are indigenous Indian regions with many people not even knowing about them.

30 species comprising of animals, birds, reptiles and amphibians are showcased in low-poly design with each one designed using 30 triangles only.The projects uses CSS properties which have not yet been standardized, limiting the best experience only to webkit browsers(mobile browsers, chrome). If you are using safari or firefox kindly switch your browser.

The data shown here is from the wikipedia pages from the respective species.
